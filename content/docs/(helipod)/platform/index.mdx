## Deployment sources

Helipod is designed to support your team's workflow, not force your team into a rigid platform flow.

- **Code Repository**: Connect a repository for automated build and deployment.
- **Container Image**: Use prebuilt images from public or private registries.
- **Template**: Start quickly with deploy-ready stacks for common use cases.

## Fast setup, deeper control

Helipod provides safe defaults so services can go live quickly. As your workload grows, you can tune build commands, start commands, resource sizing, health checks, and deployment strategy.

## Development lifecycle

Helipod goes beyond deployment and supports the full application lifecycle:

- **Configuration Management**: Manage variables and secrets per service and environment.
- **Environment Workflow**: Separate dev, staging, and production for safer releases.
- **Automation Surface**: Integrate your pipelines via CLI and API.
- **Monitoring & Diagnosis**: Access build/runtime logs and performance signals for faster troubleshooting.

## Operational model

Helipod is operated with a strong focus on reliability. The platform combines observability, alerting, and incident response procedures to keep customer workloads healthy.
